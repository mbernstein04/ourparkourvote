Why This Matters

267 Acres of Public Parkland
Are at Stake

Duluth politicians want to rezone and sell Lester Park to private developers. We believe the people should decide what happens to their land.

What's Happening

City Hall is moving forward with plans to sell 267.5 acres of public parkland at Lester Park Golf Course to private developers — without putting it to a public vote.

What We're Doing

Our Park Our Vote is collecting citizen petition signatures to force a public referendum. If we gather enough signatures, the city must let voters decide Lester's future.

How to Help

Download the petition, print it, sign it, and mail it in. Share this page with your neighbors. Every signature brings us closer to a vote.

Why It Matters

Once public land is sold, it's gone forever. This isn't about golf — it's about whether the people of Duluth get a say in what happens to land that belongs to all of us.
