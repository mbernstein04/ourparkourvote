Here's How to Make It Count

1

Download, Print & Sign

Your packet includes the signature gatherer guide and both petition sheets. Print everything, sign both petitions in ink, and bring the guide when you canvass.

Download Your Packet →
2

Canvass Your Neighborhood

Walk your street or a nearby neighborhood with petition sheets. A neighbor asking a neighbor is the most effective thing you can do. The guide has everything you need to answer questions at the door.

3

Return Completed Sheets

Mail completed sheets to Our Park, Our Vote, 1346 W. Arrowhead Rd. #324, Duluth, MN 55811 — or email info@ourparkourvote.org to arrange a drop-off.
